About Production

Helen Harper is a brand of Ontex, a leading international personal hygiene group. Ontex designs and manufactures high-quality products for Baby Care, Feminine Care, and Adult Care. We are the partner of choice for consumers, retailers, and institutional and private healthcare providers worldwide.

Helen Harper products are produced in Ontex plants in Europe, driven by ethical principles of safety, quality, and customer satisfaction. Our most valuable asset is our people, who are at the heart of everything we do.

Neom World Trade Pvt. Ltd., operating under the Pathibhara Group, is the authorized distributor of Ontex in Nepal. With a strong presence across all seven provinces of the country, Neom specializes in distributing high-quality baby care and cosmetic products, with adult care solutions set to launch soon.
