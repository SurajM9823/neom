NEOM, is for world Trade.

Neom World Trade Pvt. Ltd., operating under the Pathibhara Group, is the authorized distributor of Ontex in Nepal. With a strong presence across all seven provinces of the country, Neom specializes in distributing high-quality baby care and cosmetic products, with adult care solutions set to launch soon. Committed to excellence, Neom ensures that every product meets the highest standards of quality and hygiene, making it a trusted name for consumers nationwide. Its extensive distribution network and dedication to customer satisfaction have solidified its position as a leading player in the market. Additionally, NEOM export goods abroad, contributing to boosting the country's economy.

Pathibhara Groups: Leading with Vision and Purpose

Right from the beginning, started from the hardware business and servicing more that 12 years, incorporated as on dated 2070-05-16 as Pathibhara Hardware and suppliers pvt Itd. The name was inspired from famous temple Pathabhara Devi. Since the twelve years of business experience the Pathibhara Group has diversify business from Hardware to Hotel & restaurant and health hygiene products of global brand of Ontex and Al and innovations, The journey is successful due to its visionary leadership and professional business practices.

Our Vision, Mission & Strategy

These three key elements define the core of our company, guiding us in our journey towards excellence and success.

Vision

Our vision is to lead innovation, provide exceptional products, and create positive change in communities globally. We strive to be the top choice for customers by consistently delivering high-quality solutions that improve lives and contribute to a sustainable future.

Mission

Our mission is to enrich lives by offering reliable, high-quality products while focusing on environmental sustainability and community well-being. We aim to foster long-term relationships with our customers and partners by staying true to our values of integrity, accountability, and teamwork.

Strategy

Our strategy revolves around constant innovation, operational excellence, and expanding our global presence. We are committed to driving growth through strategic partnerships, a customer-first approach, and ensuring that sustainability is embedded in all our business practices.
