Pathibhara Groups: Leading with Vision and Purpose

Right from the beginning, started from the hardware business and servicing more that 12 years, incorporated as on dated 2070-05-16 as Pathibhara Hardware and suppliers pvt Itd. The name was inspired from famous temple Pathabhara Devi. Since the twelve years of business experience the Pathibhara Group has diversify business from Hardware to Hotel & restaurant and health hygiene products of global brand of Ontex and Al and innovations, The journey is successful due to its visionary leadership and professional business practices.

Under the Pathibhara group umbrella, we proudly manage four key organizations: Neom World Limited, a leader in delivering world-class solutions; Ontex, known for its premium-quality products; and divisions specializing in hospitality and AI technology. Together, these entities share a commitment to innovation, sustainability, and positive community impact, working towards a brighter future for all.

Hilltown Restaurant

Hilltown Restaurant is dedicated to providing a wholesome dining experience with a focus on hygienic, high-quality food. From the very beginning, we have been committed to creating a space where families and friends can gather and enjoy delicious meals in a clean, safe environment. Our dedication to maintaining the highest standards of cleanliness and food safety ensures that every dish served is both tasty and healthy.

Pathibhara Hardware & Suppliers

Pathibhara Hardware & Suppliers is a trusted name in providing high-quality hardware products and solutions across Nepal. Our company is dedicated to offering a wide range of products, from essential building materials to specialized tools, catering to both personal and industrial needs. With a focus on reliability and customer satisfaction, we ensure that every product meets the highest standards of quality and durability.

Pathibhara Group is excited to embrace the future with our ongoing plans to incorporate Artificial Intelligence (AI) and innovative technologies across various sectors. NEOM World Trade

NEOM is derived from two words: "NE" represents Neesha Chairman of NEOM World Trade, and "OM" is inspired by the sacred sound of "Om," which symbolizes purity, positivity, and a universal connection. NEOM World Trade is a trusted and authorized distributor of Ontex products, offering high-quality, innovative solutions to customers across the globe. As a company committed to excellence, we proudly represent Ontex, a leading name in personal care products, renowned for its dedication to quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction.

With a strong focus on building long-term relationships with customers and maintaining the highest standards of service, NEOM World Trade is proud to be a part of Ontex’s global network. Our commitment to delivering quality, timely services, and products has established us as a leading distributor in the industry, helping customers enjoy the best that Ontex has to offer.
