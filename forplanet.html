Sustainability / For Planet

Our Goal

Our goal is to contribute to the preservation of the planet by focusing on reducing emissions, mitigating glacier melt, and fostering sustainable practices. Here’s how we aim to make a difference:

Reducing Carbon Emissions: Carbon emissions are a significant driver of global warming, which impacts the planet. By promoting renewable energy solutions, reducing reliance on fossil fuels, and encouraging energy-efficient practices, we aim to lessen the burden on this vital region.

Addressing Glacier/ Himalaya Melt: The accelerated melting of glaciers threatens water resources, biodiversity, and livelihoods. We are committed to raising awareness about this critical issue, supporting climate resilience initiatives, and investing in research to monitor and mitigate glacier retreat.

Community Contribution and Cleanup Efforts: Preserving the planet requires collective action. We are partnering with local communities to organize large-scale cleanup campaigns, removing plastic waste and other pollutants from these pristine landscapes.

Plantation Drives for Greener Landscapes: Reforestation and afforestation are key to combating soil erosion and promoting carbon sequestration. We are spearheading plantation drives across the region, focusing on native species to restore balance and enhance biodiversity.

Our Footprint

We’re deeply committed to reducing our product’s climate footprint by gaining a thorough understanding of its entire lifecycle. This means assessing every stage—from sourcing raw materials and manufacturing to transportation, usage, and eventual disposal. By examining each step, we can better track our sustainability goals.

Neom World Trade Pvt. Ltd., operating under the Pathibhara Group, is the authorized distributor of Ontex in Nepal. With a strong presence across all seven provinces of the country, Neom specializes in distributing high-quality baby care and cosmetic products, with adult care solutions set to launch soon.
